onPlus

onPlus started it's life as a skill sharing platform named onAcademy. onAcademy platforms can be owned at an enterprise level to host other skilled people that can create and publish courses and share their knowledge with others. It can also be used as a platform to train your employees or team members. These courses then can be bought or they can be acquired for free. These courses can be live or a series of videos and documents.

onPlus takes all of these features to another level. It includes everything onAcademy has and gives you the ability to send special offers to your contacts, chat with your users and even create channels for broadcasting messages to them. After sending an offer, you can track and analyze the progress of the offer with the CRM tools onPlus provides.
